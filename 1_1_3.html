The target audience with characteristics, motivations, capabilities and devices

From the same previous step of the Ideation Cards, we could outline that the two institutions had more point of contacts in the target audience than in the assets: in fact, they share the two categories of art and animal passionate as well as the part of users enjoying collections online.
All these audiences are quite familiar with technological devices that they are used to use for several daily tasks (find an address, search for a professional, sending and receiving email and instant messaging, etc.)
On these bases, we created the Personas - as defined by the Alan Cooper method - available in the Development section.
