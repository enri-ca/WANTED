Complementary Material for the Discussion: 3d object and scenes

two different 3D models of a museum's room have been realized for this project: the first is just the room in which the paintings have been strategically positioned; the second is a sort of simulation on what could seem the virtual visit guided by a 3d model of the choosen guide, in this case we choosed Granizo, the deer that Frida adopted.

The 3D model have been realized with Blender, the annotations and the deer have been put with sketchfab; the deer, specifically, is an already created 3D model that we imported in our thanks to the open download of the resource (under the Creative Commons Copyright regulation)
